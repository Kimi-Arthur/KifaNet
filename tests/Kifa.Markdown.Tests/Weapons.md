# Weapons

[VIEW SOURCE](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Weapons?action=edit)

For a sortable list of every weapon in the game, see [List of weapons](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_weapons)
. For class setup ideas, see [Guide:Class setups](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Guide:Class_setups).

**Weapons** are essential items used for combat against [enemies](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Enemies)
, [bosses](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Bosses), critters, and even other players
during [PvP](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/PvP) games. Some weapons can be crafted at
a [Work Bench](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Work_Bench) or
a [Pre-Hardmode](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Pre-Hardmode_Anvils)/[Hardmode
Anvil](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Hardmode_Anvil), while others can only be found in Chests, as enemy drops, or
purchased from [NPCs](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/NPCs). *Terraria* has a wide variety of weapons and weapon
classes, each suited to particular play styles or specific tasks.

Most [tools](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Tools) can also be used as weapons, despite it not being their primary
purpose. In the broader sense, [traps](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Traps),
self-defending [NPCs](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/NPCs) Desktop, Console, and Mobile versions, and
even [lava](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Lava) can be used as a weapon.

### Swords

[Swords](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Swords) are melee weapons which can be divided into 2 basic categories:
shortswords and broadswords. Shortswords have very limited range, and they attack with a horizontal stabbing motion
instead of an arc.
On [![Desktop version](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/terraria_gamepedia/images/7/72/Desktop_only.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/16?cb=20180323033502)](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Desktop_version_history)[
Desktop](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Desktop_version_history)
and [![Mobile version](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/terraria_gamepedia/images/b/b2/Mobile_only.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/11?cb=20180323033433)](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Mobile_version)[
Mobile](https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Mobile_version), they act more similarly to spears, being able to stab in more
directions. Broadswords are swung overhead, which makes it easier to defeat flying and jumping enemies.

## Notes

- In total, there are 555 unique weapons (and damaging tools).
- If an enemy is under the effect of a piercing attack (including explosions), it will cause brief invulnerability to
  it, making blows that hit during this interval fail to inflict damage.
- Unlike most games, no matter how far an enemy is from the center of the blast radius, they always receive full damage
  from an AoE attack.
